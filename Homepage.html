FOODIE'S WEBSITE

We are a club that promotes the enjoyment of eating. As part of our activities, we share recipes with members and regularly conduct baking classes.

Food is a big part of our lives. During COVID-19, many people have learnt to become MCO cooks Depending on their interests, some may like pastries, savoury food, soups, breads, pastas, meat dishes and many more.

In this Foodie's Website, we help you to find the best recipes. We also have a Members' Forum where you can register for free and take part in our activities to sharpen your cooking skills.
